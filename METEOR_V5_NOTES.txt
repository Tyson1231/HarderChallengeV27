METEOR V5 - MAGMA BALL

Added:
- New MeteorShower.java
- Visible 3-D meteor made from 33 magma block-display entities
- Diagonal fall path from high in the sky
- Flame, smoke, lava trail, and incoming sound
- Impact crater lined with magma and occasional lava
- Explosion damage, knockback, particles, and thunder sound
- Supports Overworld and Nether surface targeting
- Meteors are automatically cleaned up when the event ends

Test in a world with cheats enabled:
/harderchallenge event meteor

Stop the event:
/harderchallenge event stop

NOTE:
This environment cannot download Gradle 9.5.1, so the project could not be compile-tested here.
