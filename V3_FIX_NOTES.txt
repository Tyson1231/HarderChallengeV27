HARDER CHALLENGE V3 FIXES

1. NETHER TORNADO
- Tornado no longer chooses a random coordinate that may be buried in netherrack.
- It searches up to 48 positions around the target player.
- A position is accepted only when it has a solid floor and at least 12 open blocks above it.
- It falls back near the player only if that column is also open.

2. METEOR REBUILD
- Impacts occur every 4 seconds rather than using one normal TNT every 6 seconds.
- Nether targets are found relative to the player's current cave instead of the roof heightmap.
- Each impact cuts a wide radius-6 bowl crater.
- Crater floors are lined with magma blocks, with occasional lava in the center.
- Larger flame, smoke, lava and explosion-emitter effects were added.
- Louder explosion/thunder audio and a center TNT blast add knockback/entity damage.

3. RED BLOOD-MOON SKY
- Added BloodMoonSkyMixin on the client.
- EventDirector pins the Blood Moon to midnight and thunder during the event.
- The mixin changes ClientLevel's actual returned sky color to deep pulsing red.
- fabric.mod.json now loads the client mixin configuration.
