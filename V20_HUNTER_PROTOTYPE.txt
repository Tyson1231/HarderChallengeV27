HARDER CHALLENGE V20 - HUNTER PROTOTYPE

Command:
/hc hunter spawn
/hc hunter stop
/hc hunter status

Implemented:
- One silent red message: YOU ARE BEING HUNTED.
- No Hunter sounds.
- 60-90 second stalking phase.
- Spawns 120-180 blocks away.
- Tries to remain 35-70 blocks away while stalking.
- Stops when the target is directly looking at it with line of sight.
- Attacks early if approached within 15 blocks or damaged.
- Stage clock, mutations, and normal event ticking freeze for the entire encounter.
- Any active event is stopped before the Hunter starts.
- Encounter ends when the Hunter or target player dies.
- 200 health, high armor, netherite gear, strong melee stats.

Prototype limitation:
- Uses a silent Husk body for this first gameplay test.
- The player-shaped clone renderer, the user's skin, bow switching, healing,
  advanced SmartBrainLib behaviours, and rare natural spawning come after the
  stalking/lock system is confirmed stable.

This build was not compile-verified in the assistant sandbox.
