HARDER CHALLENGE V22 - HUNTER MEMORY & PERSISTENCE

Changes:
- Hunter max health changed from 200 HP to 20 HP (normal player health).
- Hunter still uses armor/equipment for durability rather than inflated health.
- Added location memory:
  * Remembers recent places where it saw the player.
  * Searches the last known position after losing line of sight.
  * Can revisit older remembered locations instead of tracking exact coordinates through walls.
- Strengthened persistence:
  * setPersistenceRequired remains the primary anti-despawn protection.
  * Added a fallback watchdog that recreates the Hunter if the entity vanishes unexpectedly.
  * Confirmed Hunter death still ends the encounter normally.
- Replaced the chat warning with a centered subtitle shown to every connected player:
  YOU ARE BEING HUNTED.
- No sound effect was added.

This build was not compile-tested because the sandbox could not download Gradle 9.5.1.
