V27 - ADAPTIVE HUNTER CHUNK SIMULATION

- Hunter now keeps its own moving forced-chunk area so it can remain active beyond the player's simulation distance.
- Dynamic sizes:
  * 1x1 while idle/waiting
  * 3x3 while navigating, searching, hiding, or attacking
  * 5x5 maximum while stuck, building, or recovering from complex movement
- Expansion happens immediately.
- Shrinking waits 5 seconds to prevent rapid load/unload thrashing.
- Old chunks are released as the Hunter moves.
- All forced chunks are released when the Hunter encounter ends.

IMPORTANT: This build was not compile-tested in the sandbox.
