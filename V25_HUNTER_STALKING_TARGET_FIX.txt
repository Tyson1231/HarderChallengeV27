V25 Hunter stalking target fix

- Prevents the vanilla Husk target selector from overriding custom stalking behavior.
- FOLLOW_RANGE is restricted during stalking so the Husk cannot automatically acquire and chase the player.
- Any vanilla target/navigation acquired during an entity tick is cleared before custom cover/search logic runs.
- Full follow range is restored only when the Hunter enters the attacking phase.
- Watchdog replacements restore the correct follow range for the current phase.
