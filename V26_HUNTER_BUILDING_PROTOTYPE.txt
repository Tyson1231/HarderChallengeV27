V26 HUNTER BUILDING PROTOTYPE

- Fixed stalking follow range so vanilla Husk AI cannot track the player from long range.
- Fixed attack phase follow range restoration (V25 accidentally used the stalking range).
- Removed the normal stalking stuck-teleport near the player's live position.
- During stalking, a failed path is abandoned and a new cover/memory route is selected.
- Added first building behavior during ATTACKING only:
  * After being genuinely stuck, the Hunter can place cobblestone beneath itself to climb toward a higher player.
  * It can place a cobblestone support to cross the first block of a gap.
  * Placement is rate-limited and respects world border, fluids, and replaceable blocks.

This is the first building foundation, not yet full player-like construction.
