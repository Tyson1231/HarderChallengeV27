V7 CHANGES

EVOLUTION / MUTANTS
- Added native mutant variants; no external mutant-creatures mod is required.
- Mutant Zombies unlock at Stage 3.
- Mutant Skeletons unlock at Stage 5.
- Mutant Creepers unlock at Stage 7.
- Mutant Spiders unlock at Stage 9.
- Each newly encountered eligible mob rolls once for mutation.
- Mutation chance rises at later stages, capped at 24%.
- Mutants are larger, named, tougher, faster, harder-hitting, resist knockback,
  heal to their new maximum health, and emit soul-fire particles.

EARTHQUAKE
- First 4 seconds are warning-only: rumbling, dust, and light shaking.
- Seconds 4-8 intensify the shake and rattle surface blocks.
- Fissures begin only after 8 seconds.
- Cracks propagate outward in short segments instead of instantly removing the
  ground directly underneath the player.
- Added layered low-pitch rumble, movement jitter, rubble, dust and rare deep lava.
