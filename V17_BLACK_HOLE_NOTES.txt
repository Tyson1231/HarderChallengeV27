HARDER CHALLENGE V17 - BLACK HOLE EVENT

- Added BLACK_HOLE as a world event beginning at Stage 6.
- It has a 2.5% selection chance after a world event has already triggered.
- One fixed black hole forms 68-82 blocks from each player.
- It does not chase or teleport toward the player.
- Event duration: 60 seconds.
- Gravity radius grows continuously from 24 to 112 blocks.
- Deadly core grows continuously from 2.5 to 9 blocks.
- Pull force becomes stronger as the event progresses and as entities get closer.
- Players, mobs, and dropped items are pulled in.
- Dropped items are pulled especially hard.
- The black hole has a dark particle core, rotating accretion ring, and inward particle streaks.
- The action bar reports distance, current gravity radius, and remaining time.
- Entering the core causes repeated heavy damage; non-player entities are consumed.
