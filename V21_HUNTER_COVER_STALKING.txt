HARDER CHALLENGE V21 - HUNTER COVER STALKING

Changes:
- Stalking duration increased from 60-90 seconds to 6-10 minutes.
- Hunter now searches up to 28 nearby terrain positions for real line-of-sight cover.
- If the player sees the Hunter, it attempts to move behind solid blocks instead of freezing in the open.
- Hidden positions behind the player's facing direction are prioritized.
- Hunter keeps moving toward selected cover until it reaches it or the cover stops blocking sight.
- Existing event/stage lock remains active throughout stalking and combat.

Testing command:
/hc hunter spawn

NOTE: This build was not compile-tested in the sandbox. Minecraft 26.2 mapping differences may require a small API-name correction.
